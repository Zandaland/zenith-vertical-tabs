# Zenith Vertical Tabs — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 24, 2024

## Overview

Zenith Vertical Tabs is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Website content
- ❌ Personal information
- ❌ Analytics or usage data
- ❌ Cookies or tracking data
- ❌ IP addresses
- ❌ Device information

## Local Storage

The extension stores only two preferences locally on your device using Chrome's `storage.local` API:

1. Whether the sidebar is pinned open
2. Whether the onboarding tutorial has been shown

**This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.**

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why We Need It |
|------------|----------------|
| `tabs` | To display and manage your open tabs in the sidebar |
| `tabGroups` | To show and organize Chrome tab groups |
| `activeTab` | To highlight the current tab and capture previews |
| `history` | To provide URL suggestions when typing in the URL bar |
| `topSites` | To show frequently visited sites as suggestions |
| `storage` | To save your sidebar preferences locally |
| `<all_urls>` | To inject the sidebar UI into web pages |

All permissions are used solely to provide the vertical tabs functionality. **No data is transmitted to external servers.**

## Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

## Remote Code

This extension does not use any remote code. All functionality is contained within the extension package.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
